Arithmetic (Commercial Mathematics)

Interest and Finance

  • Simple Interest (S.I.): $$S.I. = \frac{P \times T \times R}{100}$$
  • Amount (A): $$A = P + S.I.$$
  • Compound Interest (C.I.): $$A = P \left(1 + \frac{R}{100}\right)^T, \quad C.I. = A - P$$
  • Profit/Loss Percentage: $$\text{Profit \%} = \frac{SP - CP}{CP} \times 100$$
  • Amount with VAT: $$\text{Amount with VAT} = SP \left(1 + \frac{\text{VAT Rate}}{100}\right)$$

Mensuration (Area, Surface Area & Volume)

3D Shapes (Prisms, Pyramids, Cones, Spheres)

  • Right Prism: $$\text{L.S.A.} = P h, \quad V = A_b \times h$$
  • Pyramid: $$\text{L.S.A.} = \frac{1}{2} P l, \quad V = \frac{1}{3} A_b \times h$$
  • Cylinder: $$\text{C.S.A.} = 2\pi r h, \quad \text{T.S.A.} = 2\pi r (h + r), \quad V = \pi r^2 h$$
  • Cone: $$\text{C.S.A.} = \pi r l, \quad V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h$$
  • Sphere: $$S.A. = 4 \pi r^2, \quad V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3$$

Matrices, Determinants and Linear Equations

  • Determinant of 2×2 Matrix: $$\det\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} = ad - bc$$
  • Inverse of 2×2 Matrix: $$A^{-1} = \frac{1}{ad-bc} \begin{pmatrix} d & -b \\ -c & a \end{pmatrix}$$
  • Condition for Unique Solution: $$\det(A) \neq 0$$

Algebra

  • Quadratic Formula: $$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$
  • Discriminant: $$\Delta = b^2 - 4ac$$
  • Factor Theorem: If $$P(a)=0$$, then $(x-a)$ is a factor of $P(x)$

Sequence and Series

  • AP: $$a_n = a + (n-1)d, \quad S_n = \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n-1)d]$$
  • GP: $$a_n = a r^{n-1}, \quad S_n = \frac{a(r^n - 1)}{r-1}, \quad S_\infty = \frac{a}{1-r} \ (|r|<1)$$

Complex Numbers

  • Standard Form: $$z = x + iy$$
  • Conjugate: $$\bar{z} = x - iy$$
  • Modulus: $$|z| = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$$
  • Polar Form: $$z = r(\cos\theta + i\sin\theta)$$
  • De Moivre's Theorem: $$(\cos \theta + i \sin \theta)^n = \cos(n\theta) + i\sin(n\theta)$$

Vectors

  • Magnitude: $$|\vec{a}| = \sqrt{a_1^2 + a_2^2 + a_3^2}$$
  • Dot Product: $$\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b} = |\vec{a}||\vec{b}|\cos\theta$$
  • Angle between Vectors: $$\cos\theta = \frac{\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b}}{|\vec{a}||\vec{b}|}$$
  • Orthogonality: $$\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b}=0$$
  • Cross Product: $$\vec{a}\times\vec{b} = \begin{vmatrix}\hat{i}&\hat{j}&\hat{k}\\a_1&a_2&a_3\\b_1&b_2&b_3\end{vmatrix}$$

Permutations and Combinations

  • Permutation: $$nP_r = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}$$
  • Combination: $$nC_r = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}$$
  • Permutation with Repetition: $$\text{Total arrangements} = \frac{n!}{n_1!n_2!\dots}$$

Probability

  • Basic Probability: $$P(A) = \frac{\text{Favorable Outcomes}}{\text{Total Outcomes}}$$
  • Addition Theorem (General): $$P(A\cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A\cap B)$$
  • Addition Theorem (Mutually Exclusive): $$P(A\cup B) = P(A) + P(B)$$
  • Multiplication Theorem: $$P(A\cap B) = P(A)\cdot P(B)$$

Co-Ordinate Geometry

  • Distance Formula: $$D = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2}$$
  • Section Formula: $$R = \left(\frac{mx_2+nx_1}{m+n}, \frac{my_2+ny_1}{m+n}\right)$$
  • Slope: $$m = \frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}$$
  • Point-Slope Line: $$y-y_1 = m(x-x_1)$$
  • Perpendicular Lines: $$m_1 m_2 = -1$$
  • General Circle Equation: $$(x-a)^2 + (y-b)^2 = r^2$$

Trigonometry

  • Compound Angle (Sine): $$\sin(A\pm B) = \sin A\cos B \pm \cos A \sin B$$
  • Double Angle (Sine): $$\sin(2A) = 2 \sin A \cos A$$
  • Double Angle (Cosine): $$\cos(2A) = \cos^2 A - \sin^2 A = 2 \cos^2 A - 1$$
  • Sine Rule: $$\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C} = 2R$$
  • General Solution: $$\theta = n\pi + (-1)^n \alpha$$

Geometry (Theorems)

  • Pythagorean Theorem: $$a^2 + b^2 = c^2$$
  • Similar Triangles (Area Ratio): $$\frac{\text{Area}_1}{\text{Area}_2} = \left(\frac{\text{Side}_1}{\text{Side}_2}\right)^2$$
  • Circle (Angle in Semicircle): 90°
